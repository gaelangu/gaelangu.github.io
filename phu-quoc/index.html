Everything else you need

The unglamorous stuff, handled — plus two checklists you can tick together.

✈️ Getting there

Scoot, VietJet and Sun PhuQuoc Airways fly Changi → Phú Quốc direct in about 2 hours, with roughly two departures a day between them. The morning rotation lands before lunch — take it.

Return fares commonly run S$180–400 pp; sale season goes lower.

🛂 Entry

Singapore passports enter Vietnam visa-free for up to 30 days. Passport valid 6+ months, return ticket handy — that’s it.

💵 Money

Vietnamese đồng. Rough maths: ₫100k ≈ S$5 · ₫1M ≈ S$52. Cards work at resorts and malls; cash rules markets, shacks and boats. ATMs cluster in Dương Đông.

🚕 Getting around

Grab (cars & bikes) works island-wide and beats haggling. Distances are real — town to the southern tip is 35–45 min. Scooters run ₫150–250k/day if you’re licensed (IDP); skip them in the rain.

🌤 Weather logic

Nov–Apr is dry season: glassy west-coast seas, peak everything. May–Oct is green season: sunny mornings, theatrical late-afternoon downpours — front-load beaches, save spa and shows for late day. Boats still run most days.

🛏 Where to sleep (3 speeds)

💎 Regent / InterContinental Long Beach — full resort mode, S$300+.
🌿 La Veranda (MGallery) — colonial-villa charm on the sand, S$180–280.
💛 Lahana Resort — hillside pools 5 min from town, S$90–150.

Base on Long Beach / Dương Đông: sunset every night, dinner walkable.

📶 Connect & plugs

Grab an eSIM (Airalo, Nomad) before you fly, or a Viettel SIM in arrivals. Power is 220V with two-pin type A/C sockets — SG plugs need an adapter.

🗣 Five phrases

xin chào — hello · cảm ơn — thank you · ngon quá! — so good! · tính tiền — bill, please · một, hai, ba, dô! — cheers!
